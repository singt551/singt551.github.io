My Movie Collection and Ratings!

A small complilation of movies that I love and have watched a few too many times! and I love to rate things, so that is included as well.

  1. Top Gun Maverick

    2. After more than 30 years of service as one of the Navy's top aviators, Pete "Maverick" Mitchell is where he belongs, pushing the envelope as a courageous test pilot and dodging the advancement in rank that would ground him. Training a detachment of graduates for a special assignment, Maverick must confront the ghosts of his past and his deepest fears, culminating in a mission that demands the ultimate sacrifice from those who choose to fly it.

    Rating

    10/10. I really enjoyed this movie. It had an equal amount of action, intellect, romance and comedy, so it was perfect.

  2. Made of Honor

    3. Always shy of commitment, Tom (Patrick Dempsey) lives as a serial dater. Hannah (Michelle Monaghan), his best friend, has wanted to marry and now has found Mr. Right -- just as Tom realizes he really loves Hannah. When she asks him to be in her bridal party, Tom seizes the opportunity to prevent the nuptials and woo her himself.

    Rating

    9/10. I liked this movie because it was a romcom. It was a bit long but overall the movie was sweet.

  3. Just my Luck

    4. As the luckiest woman in the world, Ashley (Lindsay Lohan) has a wonderful life, which all changes after she kisses a stranger (Chris Pine) at a costume party. She sees everything turn into a living hell, as she realizes that she has unwittingly exchanged her good fortune for the stranger's bad luck.

    Rating

    6/10. This movie was okay, I didn't love it. Some parts felt a bit redundant and I just didn't enjoy the music.

  4. Avengers Endgame

    5. Adrift in space with no food or water, Tony Stark sends a message to Pepper Potts as his oxygen supply starts to dwindle. Meanwhile, the remaining Avengers -- Thor, Black Widow, Captain America and Bruce Banner -- must figure out a way to bring back their vanquished allies for an epic showdown with Thanos -- the evil demigod who decimated the planet and the universe.

    Rating

    10/10. This movie was amazing. Towards the end when all the marvel characters appear, I cried tears of joy. It one of my favorite movies of all time.

  5. Life as we know it

    6. After a disastrous first date, the only things Holly Berenson (Katherine Heigl) and Eric Messer (Josh Duhamel) have in common are mutual dislike and their love for their goddaughter, Sophie. When they unexpectedly become her caretakers, Holly and Eric have to put aside their differences and learn to work together for the sake of the child. Juggling competing career and social commitments, Holly and Eric look for common ground while living under the same roof.

    Rating

    7/10. This movie was emotional and sweet, however, it could have been better. The romance was nice and the baby was cute, but I feel some parts were not needed.
